Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a renewal take?
Most renewals are completed within 5–10 working days from submission. Expedited requests may be available (contact us for details).
What if my disc is already expired?
Don't panic — we handle expired discs regularly. However, there may be penalties. Contact us immediately to renew before fines increase further.
Can you handle company vehicles?
Absolutely. For company vehicles, we'll need a BRNC (Business Registration Number Certificate), proxy ID, and proof of business address. We guide you through all of it.
Do you service all provinces?
Yes. We work with DLTCs across all South African provinces and can coordinate submissions from anywhere in the country.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, EFT, debit/credit cards, and cash (at our office). 50% deposit is required upfront; balance due on completion.
Can I track my application online?
Currently, you'll receive WhatsApp updates from us. We're building a real-time tracker app — keep an eye on our Digital Tools page for launch date.
What if there's an issue with my application?
We monitor everything closely. If an issue arises, we contact the DLTC and you immediately. We'll resolve it or propose next steps.
Do you offer a guarantee or refund?
We stand behind our work. If we make an error, we'll fix it at no extra charge. If the DLTC rejects an application due to our mistake, we refund our service fee.
